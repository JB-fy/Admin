解压安装包
    tar -xvf Clash.for.Windows-0.20.30-x64-linux.tar.gz
移动到指定目录
    mv Clash\ for\ Windows-0.20.30-x64-linux/ /server/tool/Clash-0.20.30
创建桌面快捷方式
    vi /usr/share/applications/Clash.desktop
写入
[Desktop Entry]
Name=Clash for Windows
Exec=/server/tool/Clash-0.20.30/cfw  --no-sandbox
Type=Application
StartupNotify=true
Categories=TextEditor;Development;Utility;
MimeType=text/plain;
